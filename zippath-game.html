How to Play ZipPath
1
Start at cell 1 and draw a continuous path through the grid.
2
Visit numbered cells in ascending order (1 → 2 → 3 → ...).
3
Your path must cover every cell exactly once.
4
Move horizontally or vertically — no diagonals.
5
Click to extend the path. Click the last cell to undo. Use 💡 Hint if stuck!
